Privacy Policy

Application: Otona Serum Lab Poster  ·  Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Otona Serum Lab Poster application ("the App") handles information. The App is a private, first-party automation tool operated solely by its owner to publish the owner’s own photo content to the owner’s own Instagram and TikTok accounts, and to collect performance metrics of the owner’s own posts, using the official Meta Graph API and the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. Who this App is for

The App is not a consumer product and is not offered to the public. It is used only by its owner to manage the owner’s own social media accounts. It does not create accounts for, or act on behalf of, any other user.

2. Information the App accesses

  • Account owner’s own profile information (such as user ID, account name, and avatar) retrieved through official platform APIs, used only to confirm the correct account is connected.
  • The account owner’s own content (photos and captions prepared in advance by the owner) which the App publishes to the owner’s own accounts.
  • Performance metrics of the owner’s own posts (such as reach, saved, views, likes, and comment counts) retrieved through official platform APIs.

3. Information the App does NOT collect

  • The App does not collect, process, or store the personal data of any third-party users.
  • The App does not track visitors, and this website uses no cookies or analytics.
  • The App does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for advertising.

4. Storage and security

Access tokens used to connect to Instagram and TikTok are stored only as private environment variables / platform secrets on the owner’s own machine or private repository. They are never displayed publicly, embedded in code, or shared. Performance metrics of the owner’s own posts are stored locally by the owner for the owner’s own review.

5. Third-party platforms

The App interacts with Instagram (Meta) and TikTok through their official APIs. Use of those platforms is subject to their respective privacy policies and terms: Instagram/Meta Privacy Policy and TikTok Privacy Policy.

6. Data retention and deletion

Because the App only handles the account owner’s own data, the owner may delete stored metrics or revoke the App’s access tokens at any time through the respective platform’s settings, which immediately ends the App’s access.

7. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
